YouTube is expanding its Primetime Channels lineup with the addition of FOX One, now available, and Peacock, coming soon. Whether viewers are looking forward to the FIFA World Cup 2026 or the latest from Bravo, the additions bring more live and on-demand premium content directly into the YouTube experience. The move continues YouTube's strategy of consolidating streaming channels within its platform.
