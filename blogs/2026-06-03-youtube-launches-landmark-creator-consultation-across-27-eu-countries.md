Across Europe, a thriving ecosystem of creators is shaping modern culture and generating significant economic value, but the creator economy has not yet reached its full potential. YouTube is launching a consultation across all 27 EU countries to hear directly from creators about the barriers they face and the opportunities they see for success. The initiative is described as a landmark effort to incorporate creator voices into policy and platform decisions.
